The server rules

We know it's boring, but in order to remain family friendly, we have to lay down some ground rules.

No Tolerance Policy

The following rules, if broken, will result in an instant ban with no exceptions.

Slurs and Hate Speech
Bullying, violence, and/or harassment
NSFW discussions or content of any kind
Doxxing or Raiding for any reason
Using alt accounts to evade bans
Posting links containing scams or viruses
Posting anything illegal
Violation of Discord's Terms of Service

We use logging bots on our server. We will know if you're trying to avoid these rules. No second chances will be given. Just follow the rules and you will be fine.

Social Principal Policy

The following rules, if broken, will not result in an instant ban. However, repeat offenders will be treated on a case-by-case basis.

Encouraging piracy or sharing links to counterfeit products
Encouraging bullying or harassment
Disrespecting staff
Promoting other servers either in-server or via DMs
Swearing, political anything, or religion (unless in context of religion in the Blueyverse, ie Flatpack)
Spamming (including repeated pings)
Posting random memes/gifs that have nothing to do with the conversation.
Having unrelated discussions that don't belong in that channel/thread
Engaging in heated debates, controversial discussions, or venting
Excessive discussion of drama and/or past trolls
Discussing content not yet released on Disney+ outside of bluey-spoilers
Non-family-friendly content
Roleplaying (show or otherwise)
Shipping of characters who are minors. This includes discussion of the topic and media including fanfiction, art, etc
Excessive discussion or promotion of substance use, including alcohol, cannabis, etc.

We are an Engish language only server to make moderation jobs easier on our staff.

Our moderation team reserves the right to revoke your access to The Heeler House and associated services for any reason we deem necessary.
